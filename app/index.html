CME 161: Interactive Data Visualization Spring 2016

Project

In this visualization, we investigated shot selections and the shooting performance of 30 NBA Teams in 2015~2016 regular season. The data came from NBA.com/Stats. The data has shooting information by distance and by month for each team. We added team information (conference, playoff/non-playoff, regular season wins). The library used are: D3.js (3.5.17) and DC.js (1.7.5) .
